Starting a club is really simple, here's what you need to do!
  1. Have an idea for a club. Be sure that it doesn't copy another club and it's something that people could be interested in!
  2. Find a club advisor. The club advisor's job is a teacher at Rocklin, who's room you will use to host your club meetings. Ask a teacher!
  3. Fill out a club form or something
