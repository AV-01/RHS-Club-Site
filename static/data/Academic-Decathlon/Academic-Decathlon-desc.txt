The Academic Decathlon Club at Rocklin High School is a dynamic and intellectually stimulating organization that brings together students with a passion for learning, critical thinking, and academic excellence. Comprising a diverse group of motivated individuals, this club provides a unique platform for students to showcase their talents across a wide range of academic disciplines.
<br>
As a member of the Academic Decathlon Club, students engage in a comprehensive and challenging curriculum that spans literature, mathematics, science, social studies, art, music, economics, and essay writing. The club's primary goal is to foster a love for learning while honing essential skills such as research, public speaking, and teamwork.
<br>
Last year, we won at regionals and moved on to state, with some members even moving on to nationals!
